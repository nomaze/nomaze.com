Why are we building this?

At NoMaze, we recognize that plant breeders lay the critical foundation upon which the future of sustainable food production and research is built.

State-of-the-art technologies like genetic prediction, advanced phenotyping, and machine learning have the potential to revolutionize the whole breeding process.
Unfortunately, many of these techniques are only usable by technologists and global corporates.

Our mission is to democratize such high-tech tools, empowering plant scientists and breeders in SMEs to focus on their most vital work: discovering the best pedigrees.

Frequently asked questions

Have other questions? Get in touch with our team via [email protected]

▼ What is NoMaze?

NoMaze is a web-based software that integrates with your existing Database. On the website you will find interactive visualizations that help you understand your data intuitively.We constantly improve the software to better suit your needs and to help you find the most interesting correlations.

▼ Why plant breeding?

Candidate selection is one of the most important and impactful steps in the creation of better and safer plants for both human and animal food supply. We see unique challenges in the process of interpreting the data generated in the process and want to contribute to the global advancement of plant research.

▼ Does it work on iPhones?

As a web-app you can access NoMaze on any device with a web browser. However the current version is optimized for use on Desktop computers and it is recommended to use a screen with a 4:3 ratio or wider.

▼ Who is it for?

NoMaze is currently designed specifically around the needs of plant breeders looking for the best candidates in their experiments.Therefore many preconfigured visualizations are only valid in the domain of phenotypic data eg. from a Maize population.However we plan to generalize the software to other use cases in the future - if you think we should consider your data first, please contact us at [email protected]!

▼ How can I reach you?

We're always available to answer any questions via mail at [email protected]. You can also directly schedule a meeting with us using the blue button in the bottom right corner of the website.

▼ Do you have international support?

NoMaze currently operates from Munich, Germany. Therefore we offer support in both german and english language.

▼ Who is behind NoMaze?

The company NoMaze was founded straight out of university by two students of the Technical University Munich. Please have a look at our About page for more information.
