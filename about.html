Our vision

At NoMaze we accelerate plant breeding research through AI.
We believe that data should bring answers and not questions, therefore we build intuitive data understanding tools to help domain experts get new insights from their existing data.
We leverage state-of-the-art ML to predict how plants will look like from their DNA, saving breeders both time and money, because unpromising candidates can be discarded early on.
